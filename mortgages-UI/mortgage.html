Why Do I need Mortgage cover?

A mortgage is a big commitment for anyone, and the unpredictability of life means that mortgage cover is wise. In the event of losing your job or becoming incapacitated through illness or injury, you need to think about whether you could continue to meet your mortgage payment obligations.

UK Finance reported there were around 90,700 mortgages in arrears in 2023 and you risk losing your home if you cannot keep up with the repayments.

Mortgage Cover Allows You To Safeguard Yourself And Your Family Against The Impact And Stress Of These Eventualities.
